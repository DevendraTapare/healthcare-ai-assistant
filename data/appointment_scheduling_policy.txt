APPOINTMENT SCHEDULING POLICY
Mindbowser Health Clinic — Patient Access Services
Document Version: 2.1 | Effective Date: March 2024

This policy governs all appointment scheduling activities at Mindbowser Health
Clinic, including in-person consultations, specialist referrals, follow-up
visits, and telehealth sessions.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO SCHEDULE AN APPOINTMENT
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Patients may book appointments through the following channels:

1. Patient Portal: Visit portal.mbhealthclinic.com and log in with your
   registered credentials. Select "Book Appointment," choose your preferred
   department and physician, and pick an available time slot. Confirmation
   is sent via email and SMS within 5 minutes.

2. Phone: Call our scheduling desk at +91-20-6789-0000, available Monday to
   Saturday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM IST. After-hours urgent appointments can be
   arranged by calling our 24-hour nurse helpline at +91-20-6789-0100.

3. In-Person: Visit the Registration Desk on the ground floor of the clinic.
   Walk-in scheduling is available subject to physician availability.

4. Referral Appointments: If you have been referred by another physician,
   present your referral letter at the Registration Desk or upload it through
   the Patient Portal. Referral appointments are prioritised and typically
   confirmed within 24 business hours.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ADVANCE BOOKING WINDOW
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Routine appointments may be booked up to 60 days in advance. Follow-up
appointments for existing patients may be booked up to 90 days in advance.
Same-day appointments are available on a limited, first-come-first-served
basis for urgent non-emergency cases and can be requested via phone or
in person starting at 8:00 AM each day.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
REQUIRED INFORMATION AT BOOKING
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

To complete your appointment booking, you will need to provide:

- Full legal name (as per government-issued photo ID)
- Date of birth
- Registered mobile number and email address
- Reason for visit or primary complaint (brief description)
- Insurance provider name and policy number (if applicable)
- Referral letter or previous prescription (for specialist consultations)

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CANCELLATION AND RESCHEDULING POLICY
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Patients may cancel or reschedule appointments at no charge up to 4 hours
before the scheduled time. Cancellations made within 4 hours of the appointment
time may incur a late cancellation fee of ₹500, which will be applied to
the patient's account. This fee is waived for documented medical emergencies
with appropriate verification.

Patients who do not attend their appointment and do not cancel in advance
(no-show) will be charged a no-show fee of ₹750. Repeated no-shows (three
or more within a 12-month period) may result in restrictions on advance
booking privileges.

To cancel or reschedule, use the Patient Portal, call the scheduling desk,
or send a request to appointments@mbhealthclinic.com at least 4 hours prior.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
APPOINTMENT REMINDERS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Automated reminders are sent 48 hours and 2 hours before your appointment
via SMS and email. Patients are encouraged to confirm their appointment by
replying "YES" to the SMS reminder or clicking the confirmation link in the
email. Failure to confirm does not cancel the appointment.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WAIT TIMES AND DELAYS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Mindbowser Health Clinic aims to see all patients within 15 minutes of their
scheduled appointment time. In the event of a delay exceeding 30 minutes,
staff will notify waiting patients and offer the option to reschedule without
penalty. Emergencies in the department may occasionally cause unavoidable delays.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SPECIAL SCHEDULING REQUIREMENTS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Patients requiring wheelchair access, sign language interpretation, or other
accessibility accommodations must inform the scheduling team at the time of
booking. Interpreter services are available in Marathi, Hindi, and English
at no additional charge. Accommodation requests made less than 24 hours before
the appointment may not be fulfilled.
