Q2: HOW DO I VERIFY MY INSURANCE ELIGIBILITY BEFORE MY VISIT?
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

You can verify your eligibility in three ways:

1. Patient Portal: Log in at portal.mbhealthclinic.com, navigate to
   "Insurance & Billing," and enter your policy number. Real-time eligibility
   status is displayed if your insurer participates in our e-verification system.

2. Billing Desk: Visit or call our Billing Department at +91-20-6789-0200,
   Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST. Have your insurance card ready.

3. Insurer Directly: Call the customer care number on the back of your
   insurance card. Ask specifically whether Mindbowser Health Clinic, Baner,
   Pune is an empanelled network provider for your policy.

Pre-authorisation may be required for elective procedures, surgeries, and
extended inpatient stays. Pre-auth requests typically take 24 to 72 business
hours to process. Planned admissions should initiate pre-authorisation at
least 3 working days before the procedure date.

Q3: WHAT IS A CO-PAYMENT AND WILL I NEED TO PAY ONE?
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

A co-payment (co-pay) is the fixed portion of your medical bill that you
pay directly, with the remainder covered by your insurer. Co-pay amounts
vary by policy type. Common co-pay structures seen at our clinic:

- 0% co-pay: Many comprehensive corporate group policies
- 10–20% co-pay: Most individual health insurance plans
- 30% co-pay: Some senior citizen or top-up policies
- Fixed amount co-pay: Government scheme beneficiaries may pay a fixed
  flat fee per visit or per admission

Your Explanation of Benefits (EOB) document from your insurer details your
specific co-pay obligations. If you are unsure, contact your insurer before
your appointment to avoid unexpected out-of-pocket expenses.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q4: WHAT DOCUMENTS MUST I BRING FOR CASHLESS TREATMENT?
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

For cashless treatment (where the insurer pays the clinic directly), bring:

- Original insurance card (physical or digital)
- Government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, passport, or driving licence)
- Insurer's pre-authorisation letter (for planned admissions and surgeries)
- Referral letter from your primary physician (for specialist consultations
  that require referral under your policy terms)
- Previous discharge summary or prescription (for follow-up visits)

Failure to produce required documents at the time of registration may result
in the cashless facility being denied and full payment being required upfront,
with reimbursement claimed from the insurer at a later date.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q5: WHAT IF MY CLAIM IS DENIED?
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

If your insurance claim is denied, you have the right to appeal the decision.
Common reasons for denial include policy exclusions (pre-existing condition
waiting periods, cosmetic procedures), missing documentation, or out-of-network
provider status. Our billing team will provide you with the denial reason code
and assist in preparing an appeal letter. Most insurers require appeals to be
submitted within 30 days of the denial notice.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q6: HOW LONG DOES CLAIM PROCESSING TAKE?
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Cashless claims settled directly with the insurer are typically processed
within 4 to 6 hours for daycare procedures and 24 to 48 hours for inpatient
admissions. Reimbursement claims submitted by patients directly to the insurer
typically take 15 to 30 business days. Delays may occur if documentation is
incomplete or if the claim requires medical review.
